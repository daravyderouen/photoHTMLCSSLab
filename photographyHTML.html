I was born in a Thailand refugee camp. The only photography my parents have of me before we came
to America is of this one. If you're wondering which one am I, I'm the smallest
one in the photo.I know very little of my past. I often envy my friends who have ablums of
family pictures to look at. It wasn't until my father passed away in 2002
& we had to find pictures of him that we barley had any to choose from. We didn't even own a camera!
That is how my journey with photography began...


A picture is worth a thousand words.


Lasting Impressions




Why photography? Why not?!

  • Document a very important event in your life.
  • Very affordable.
  • Make great gifts.
  • Passing on moment in history.
  • SAVE MONEY BY TAKING IT YOURSELF(You might regret this one when you're taking family photos.)


What do you need to start?

  1. At least one eye that can see, that's a must!
  2. At least one hand, or finger.
  3. A camera! (Whether it's on your phone, manual or DSLR)

That's it! That is all you need!



Photography is a gift that keeps giving.
